# AnEnemy — itch.io page copy

## Tagline

Two anemone clones, one rock. Crank to engorge your venom tentacles and lash.

## Description

AnEnemy is a real-time, crank-native dueling game about sea-anemone warfare.
Aggregating anemones live in genetically identical clones, and where two clones
meet they hold a no-man's-land and fight in ranks with **acrorhagi** — venom
tentacles used only against rivals. You command the front-line polyps. Crank
forward to engorge your acrorhagi, then release with Ⓐ to lash across the gap;
reach and sting both scale with how hard you charged, and a lash only connects
if it spans the no-man's-land. But engorging stops your feeding and burns
energy, so every wind-up is a gamble: over-attack and you starve, over-feed and
the boundary caves in on you.

Damage isn't a health bar — it's **sting**, stipple scars accreting on the oral
disc until the rival crosses its tolerance, deflates, and cedes the cell. Win
skirmishes to push the boundary across a rock of cells; held territory
reinforces your next duel, so a lead snowballs until you clear the rock. Then a
new rival strain claims the next one — a glass-cannon Actinia, an
endure-everything Metridium, a resistant Urticina, and a nudibranch boss — up a
four-strain campaign ladder, all under a tide that bares the rock at low water
and sends a diving gull and a grazing sea slug across it. 1-bit, procedural art
and pure synthesized audio, no asset files.

## Features

- Crank-native combat: engorge the acrorhagi, time the lash, learn the
  connect-reach threshold
- Feed-vs-fight economy — charging stops your feeding and burns energy
- Damage as accreting sting scars against a tolerance, not a health bar
- A full tide clock: contract, heal and desiccate on the bared rock at low water
- Two predators — a diving gull and an Aeolidia nudibranch — that maul either clone
- Territory war: push a boundary across a rock of cells; held ground reinforces you
- A four-strain campaign ladder, each rival with its own fighting personality
- Tide-phase step-sequencer music and synth SFX; records that persist between sessions

## Controls

- **Crank forward** — engorge your acrorhagi (charge → reach + sting)
- **Crank back** — deflate the charge / bail out
- **Ⓐ** — lash with the current charge; confirm on menus

## Install (no dev tools needed)

checkout), then sideload it at <https://play.date/account/sideload/> — or unzip
it and open the `.pdx` in the Playdate Simulator.
